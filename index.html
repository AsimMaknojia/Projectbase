CFS® & CAS®

The Certified Fund Specialist® designation was developed in 1988 by The Institute Of Business &Finance. It is the fourth-oldest designation in the U.S. CFS® designated advisors maintain a strong working knowledge of mutual funds, variable sub-accounts, and Exchange Traded Funds. CFS® advisors practice advanced fund analysis and selection, asset allocation and portfolio construction. The CFS® training allows advisors to master sophisticated investment strategies that are utilized on a daily basis for risk management, taxes, and estate planning.

A Certified Annuity Specialist® is an educated, experienced and qualified professional committed to the well being of you, your family, and your business. The CAS® can provide basic and advanced guidance on a range of actively and passively managed annuities. The process used by the CAS® involves data gathering as well as advanced knowledge of over three dozen investment categories, performance and risk evaluation, asset allocation, tax efficiency, and retirement plans. The CAS® has the ability to enhance your retirement by considering investments with guarantees, balance for risk adjusted return, and tax-advantaged income streams for life.
